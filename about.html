About Tendway

Reliable rides, delivered with care.

Tendway was founded on a simple belief: getting to a medical appointment shouldn't be the hardest part of someone's day.

For many people across the Greater Toronto Area — seniors, those recovering from procedures, or anyone without a ride — a missed appointment can mean a missed treatment. We exist to close that gap with dependable, comfortable, non-emergency medical transportation.

We focus on ambulatory and seated transport: private, door-to-door rides for clients who can walk and get in and out of a vehicle with little or no assistance. No shared shuttle chaos, no ambulance price tag — just a friendly driver and a clean, comfortable vehicle that gets you where you need to be, on time.

Every trip is built around three things we never compromise on: punctuality, safety, and genuine, compassionate service.
