Our Services

Seated, ambulatory transport designed around your schedule and comfort.

Who we serve

Ambulatory & seated transport

Tendway is ideal for clients who can walk and enter/exit a vehicle with little or no assistance. Comfortable, private rides — not a shared shuttle, not an ambulance.

🏥

Medical Appointments

Round-trip rides to doctors, specialists, clinics, imaging, and follow-up visits. We can wait or return on schedule so you're never stranded.

💉

Dialysis & Treatment

Dependable recurring transport for dialysis, chemotherapy, radiation, and physiotherapy. Set up a repeating schedule and we'll be there.

🏠

Hospital & Clinic Discharge

A calm, comfortable ride home when you're discharged — so a family member doesn't have to drop everything to pick you up.

🔁

Recurring Rides

Regular standing appointments? Book a repeating schedule once and let us handle the logistics every week.

🛒

Errands & Personal Trips

Pharmacy runs, lab work, and essential personal appointments. Door-to-door service at a relaxed, unhurried pace.

👥

Companion-Friendly

A family member or caregiver is welcome to ride along to appointments at no hassle — just let us know when booking.
