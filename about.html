About Tendway

Reliable rides, delivered with genuine care.

Why we started Tendway

We believe getting to a medical appointment shouldn't be the hardest part of someone's day.

For many people across the GTA — seniors, those recovering from procedures, or anyone without a ride — a missed appointment can mean a missed treatment. Tendway exists to close that gap with dependable, comfortable, non-emergency medical transportation.

We focus on ambulatory and seated transport: private, door-to-door rides for clients who can walk and get in and out of a vehicle with little or no assistance. No shared-shuttle chaos, no ambulance price tag — just a friendly driver and a clean, comfortable vehicle.
